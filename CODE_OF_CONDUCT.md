# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to making participation in this project a respectful, harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive in discussions.
- Assume good intent and ask clarifying questions before escalating.
- Accept feedback professionally.
- Focus critique on ideas and implementation, not individuals.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, discrimination, or hate speech.
- Personal attacks, insults, or derogatory language.
- Publishing private information without explicit permission.
- Repeatedly derailing technical discussions in bad faith.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies in all project spaces, including issues, pull requests, discussions, and other community channels.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing this Code of Conduct. They may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, issues, and other contributions that violate this policy.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, report it to the maintainers through private channels available for this repository. Include links, screenshots, and context when possible.

All reports will be reviewed and handled as promptly and fairly as possible.

## Consequences

Maintainers may take any action they deem appropriate, including warnings, temporary restrictions, or permanent bans from project spaces.
